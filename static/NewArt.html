Watercolor Works

When I travel, one of my favorite mediums is watercolor. In some instances, the water I use is from the location I paint. I try to keep my watercolor illlustrations loose, comfortable, and inviting.

Digital Illustrations

Whether Commisioned or not, I strive to remember that we live in a digital age. Therefore, I push my self to create digital art through photoshop and illustrator.

Acrylic Paint

Due to our relocation to Romania in late 2017, I have become a friend of this easy to clean and easy to purchase medium. I use this art as a stress reliever, so these works some in seasons and are uncommissioned.

Branding Design

This section of my portfolio includes some of my highlighted logos, branding updates, websites, marketing campaigns and other projects that would fall under the branding side of graphic design.

Photography

Like most of my other mediums, I can see a theme this year due to our relocation to Europe. Most of my Photography falls under the catagory of travel. I have a BFA from UTA with a concentration in Photography. In and right after college I focused on and loved studio photography. Now I am happy to say, I am finding my education extreemly helpful as I learn to enjoy landcape, travel and cultural portrait photography as well. I hope you enjoy.
